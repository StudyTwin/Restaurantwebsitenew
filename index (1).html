Our Story

Three Generations, One Table

Golden Fork Bistro opened its doors in 1995, when chef Antonio Reyes and his wife Elena turned a small corner storefront into a neighborhood gathering place — built on the belief that a great meal starts with honest, fresh ingredients and ends with people lingering long after the plates are cleared.

Today, their daughter Camila leads the kitchen, blending her parents' recipes with her own classical training to create a menu that feels both familiar and quietly inventive. Every dish that leaves our kitchen still follows the family's first rule: nothing reaches the table unless it would be served at our own family dinner.

— The Reyes Family
